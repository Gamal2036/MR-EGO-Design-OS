- Centralized animation controller with queue management

---

## Expansion Rules

1. Any new motion or interaction must pass the Design Principles checklist (DP-0)
2. New motion must not contradict existing motion specifications
3. New interactions must have reduced-motion and accessibility alternatives
4. All new motion must use the timing and easing systems defined in [Motion-System.md](Motion-System.md)
5. Performance budgets apply to all new motion — 60fps minimum
