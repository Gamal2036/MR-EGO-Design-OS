# Brand Constitution

**Phase:** DP-0 (Foundation)
**Version:** 1.0
**Status:** FOUNDATION — Permanent
**Authority:** Defines the brand identity, voice, and visual direction for MR:EGO.

---

## Brand Personality

MR:EGO is confident without arrogance, intelligent without pretension, and ambitious without aggression.

The personality is best described as:

- **Professional:** Every interaction belongs in a premium workplace environment.
- **Empathetic:** The platform understands the user's goals and challenges.
- **Calm:** Never urgent, never panicked, never demanding. MR:EGO waits patiently.
- **Precise:** Language is exact. Information is accurate. Design is intentional.
- **Forward-thinking:** The perspective is always on growth, evolution, and future potential.
- **Trustworthy:** Reliability is expressed in every interaction, every data point, every response time.

## Brand Voice

The voice of MR:EGO is **direct, warm, and authoritative**. It speaks to professionals as equals — never talking down, never over-explaining, never assuming ignorance.

- **Direct:** Clear, concise sentences. No fluff, no filler.
- **Warm:** Human tone with appropriate warmth. Never robotic, never overly casual.
- **Authoritative:** Speaks with earned confidence based on data and thoughtful analysis.
- **Encouraging:** Supports the user's ambitions without false positivity.
- **Transparent:** Explains why things happen, what data is used, and what will happen next.

### Voice Rules

1. Prefer active voice: "MR:EGO identified 3 opportunities" not "3 opportunities were identified by MR:EGO."
2. Use plain language: "start" not "initiate," "use" not "utilize," "help" not "facilitate."
3. Address the user as "you," refer to MR:EGO as "MR:EGO" or "we" in appropriate contexts.
4. Never use jargon, buzzwords, or industry cliches ("synergy," "leverage," "circle back").
5. When presenting AI-generated content, always indicate the source: "Based on your recent activity, MR:EGO suggests..."

## Brand Tone

Tone adapts to context while remaining consistent with the brand voice.

| Context | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| Onboarding | Encouraging, guiding | "Let's set up your workspace. It takes 2 minutes." |
| Decision Support | Neutral, informative | "Based on your profile, these 3 roles are strong matches." |
| Error Condition | Calm, helpful | "This action couldn't be completed. Here's what happened and how to fix it." |
| Success State | Celebratory, measured | "Your profile is complete. You're ready for your next opportunity." |
| AI Suggestions | Deferential, clear | "MR:EGO suggests reviewing this opportunity. You have full control." |
| Critical Actions | Serious, precise | "This will permanently delete your workspace data. Confirm to proceed." |
| Notifications | Brief, actionable | "Your monthly career review is ready. View summary." |

## Professional Image

MR:EGO presents as a premium professional tool, not a consumer application, not a social platform, not a game.

- Visual design communicates **competence and reliability**.
- Every surface appears **well-crafted and intentional**.
- The brand belongs in boardrooms, personal studies, and enterprise dashboards.
- Marketing materials reflect the same quality as the product interface.
- Partnerships and integrations are displayed with the same care as core features.

## Innovation

Innovation in MR:EGO is **purposeful**, not experimental.

- New features solve clearly defined user problems.
- AI capabilities are introduced when they demonstrably improve decision quality or reduce friction.
- Visual innovation is expressed through interaction quality, not visual novelty.
- Technical innovation happens beneath the surface — the user experiences the benefit, not the mechanism.

## Trust

Trust is the foundation of the MR:EGO brand. Every design decision either builds or damages trust.

- **Data transparency:** Show the user exactly what data is stored, used, and shared.
- **Privacy clarity:** Privacy controls are visible and adjustable from any screen.
- **AI honesty:** Label AI-generated content clearly. Never impersonate human judgment.
- **System reliability:** Uptime, response times, and data integrity are guaranteed and communicated.
- **Accountability:** Every action is logged. Every decision is reversible where possible.

## Premium Experience

Premium is not about decoration — it is about **absence of friction** and **presence of quality**.

- Interactions are fluid, responsive, and predictable.
- Loading states are elegant. Errors are graceful. Empty states are informative.
- Typography is spacious. Colors are restrained. Motion is purposeful.
- Every pixel has a reason. Every animation has meaning. Every word has purpose.

## Modern Technology

MR:EGO embraces modern technology while maintaining timeless design.

- The platform is responsive-first and works across all device classes.
- Dark mode and light mode are both first-class citizens.
- Accessibility features are built in, not bolted on.
- Performance targets: initial load under 2 seconds, interaction under 100ms.
- Progressive Web App capabilities where native features are not required.

## Calm Experience

MR:EGO is a calm technology. It serves the user without demanding their attention.

- Notifications are meaningful and actionable, never promotional.
- The platform works in the background and surfaces only when value is added.
- No badges, no alerts, no red dots for non-critical items.
- The user controls notification frequency, channels, and content.
- MR:EGO respects focus time and does not interrupt without cause.

Refer to [UX-Constitution.md](UX-Constitution.md) for detailed experience rules.

## Visual Identity

MR:EGO's visual identity is **minimal, structured, and confident**.

- **Clean layouts** with generous whitespace and clear information hierarchy.
- **Grid-based design** ensures consistency across all screens and surfaces.
- **Restrained use of color** — color carries meaning, not decoration.
- **High contrast** for readability and accessibility.
- **Consistent spacing** following an 8px grid system.

## Typography Direction

- **Primary typeface:** A modern, geometric sans-serif with excellent readability at all sizes (e.g., Inter, SF Pro, or equivalent).
- **Monospace:** For code, data, and structured content where alignment is critical.
- **Hierarchy:** Clear size, weight, and color differentiation between headings, body, and supporting text.
- **Scale:** Limited, intentional type scale based on a 1.25 modular scale (major third).
- **Line height:** Generous leading (1.5x body, 1.2x headings) for readability.
- **Maximum line length:** 70 characters for body text to maintain comfortable reading.

Detailed typography specifications, including exact families, weights, and sizes, are defined in the Design System (DP-2).

## Spacing Philosophy

- **8px base unit:** All spacing, padding, margins, and layout distances are multiples of 8px.
- **4px micro-unit** permitted for dense content and compact views.
- **Consistent density** within each context — similar content uses identical spacing.
- **Whitespace is a design element:** Empty space is as important as filled space.
- **Breathing room** between interactive elements prevents errors and improves usability.

## Motion Philosophy

Motion in MR:EGO is purposeful and restrained.

- **Duration:** Short (150–300ms) for functional animations, longer (300–500ms) for transitional animations.
- **Easing:** Natural easing curves (ease-in-out, deceleration) — never linear.
- **Purpose:** Motion communicates hierarchy, state change, and spatial relationships.
- **Reduction:** Users with motion sensitivity can reduce or disable motion.
- **No decoration:** If animation does not communicate, it does not exist.
- **No parallax, no marquees, no auto-playing content.**

## Color Philosophy

Color serves function, not fashion.

- **Primary palette:** A single accent color used sparingly for primary actions and key interactive elements.
- **Neutral palette:** Extensive grayscale for structure, text, backgrounds, and surfaces.
- **Semantic colors:** Distinct, accessible colors for success, warning, error, and information states.
- **Accessibility first:** All color combinations meet WCAG AA contrast ratios (minimum 4.5:1 for text).
- **Theme support:** Light and dark themes are both fully designed, not afterthoughts.
- **Limited palette:** Fewer colors reduce cognitive load and create stronger visual identity.

Exact color values, swatches, and usage rules are defined in the Design System (DP-2).

## Icon Philosophy

- **Purpose over decoration:** Every icon communicates a concept. Icons are never used as decoration.
- **Consistent style:** All icons share the same stroke weight, corner radius, and visual density.
- **Labeled by default:** Icons include text labels unless the icon is universally understood and space is constrained.
- **Accessible:** Icons include proper aria labels and alt text.
- **Simple:** No complex illustrations. Icons are minimal, clear, and recognizable at 16–24px.
- **Custom set:** MR:EGO uses a custom icon set for brand consistency.
