# Project Constitution

**Phase:** DP-0 (Foundation)
**Version:** 1.0
**Status:** FOUNDATION — Permanent
**Authority:** Highest design authority for MR:EGO

---

## Vision

MR:EGO empowers every professional to build, manage, and evolve their career through intelligent workspace technology that adapts to the individual. We envision a world where career growth is guided, measurable, and continuously aligned with personal aspirations and market reality.

## Mission

Deliver a unified, intelligent platform that combines workspace management, career development, and AI-powered decision support into a single cohesive experience — eliminating the fragmentation of tools professionals currently juggle.

## Purpose

Professionals deserve a single source of truth for their work life. MR:EGO exists to replace the patchwork of disconnected tools with one integrated system that understands who you are, where you want to go, and how to get you there.

## Long-term Vision

MR:EGO evolves from a personal workspace and career companion into an ecosystem that connects professionals, teams, organizations, and AI agents. Over the next decade, MR:EGO becomes the standard operating system for professional life — encompassing workspace, career, learning, collaboration, and marketplace functions, all governed by the same design philosophy and technical architecture defined in this Constitution.

## Product Philosophy

Every product decision must answer one question: **Does this reduce friction for the professional?** Features exist to eliminate complexity, not add it. If a feature does not make the user's professional life simpler, it does not belong in MR:EGO.

MR:EGO is a tool of augmentation, not replacement. The product enhances human capability through intelligent assistance while keeping the human firmly in control of every decision that matters.

## Engineering Philosophy

Build for permanence. Every line of code, every API contract, every data structure must assume it will exist for a decade. Short-term convenience never justifies long-term debt.

- **Modularity over Monoliths:** Systems are independently deployable, testable, and replaceable.
- **Simplicity over Cleverness:** The simplest solution that meets requirements is the correct one.
- **Consistency over Novelty:** Use established patterns unless a new pattern provides disproportionate value.
- **Testability over Faith:** Every component must be verifiable in isolation.
- **Performance over Premature Optimization:** Correct first, then fast.
- **Security by Design:** Security is not a layer; it is inherent in every architectural decision.

## Design Philosophy

Design is not decoration. Design is the elimination of friction between the user and their goal.

- **Minimalism is not emptiness:** It is the precise presence of exactly what is needed.
- **Elegance is efficiency:** The shortest path to the user's goal is the most elegant.
- **Premium is attention to detail:** Every pixel, every animation, every micro-interaction has intent.
- **Timelessness over Trends:** Design decisions must age gracefully. Avoid stylistic fads.
- **Accessibility is not a constraint:** It is a design requirement equal to functionality.

Refer to [Design-Principles.md](Design-Principles.md) for the full design rulebook.

## Human Approval Philosophy

MR:EGO never takes autonomous action on behalf of the user without explicit, informed consent for actions with lasting consequence.

- **Guardian AI** observes, suggests, warns, and informs — but never executes without approval.
- **Decision Support** presents options with evidence, but the human chooses.
- **Execution** of approved actions is the only automated phase, and even then reversibility is preferred.
- **Critical paths** (job applications, data deletion, financial commitments) always require explicit human confirmation.
- The human remains the **principal**, MR:EGO is always the **agent**.

## AI Philosophy

Artificial intelligence in MR:EGO serves three distinct roles, clearly separated by design:

1. **Observant — The Guardian:** Monitors context, identifies patterns, surfaces insights. Never intrusive, always available.
2. **Advisory — The Provider:** Offers recommendations, generates options, forecasts outcomes. Presents choices without coercion.
3. **Executive — The Executioner:** Performs only pre-approved, reversible, well-scoped actions under human supervision.

AI is transparent in its reasoning, honest about its confidence, and deferential to human judgment. AI actions are always auditable. AI decisions are always explainable. AI failures are always graceful.

## Core Values

| **Trust First** | Every interaction builds or erodes trust. Design for the former. |
| **Human Centered** | Technology serves people, not the reverse. |

| **Quality Without Compromise** | If it is not excellent, it is not shipped. |

| **Inclusive by Default** | Accessibility and inclusion are not features; they are requirements. |

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **User Activation** | Percentage of new users who reach their first meaningful workflow milestone within 7 days. |
| **Task Completion Rate** | Percentage of initiated workflows completed without error or abandonment. |
| **Time-to-Value** | Time from first login to first delivered insight or completed task. |
| **User Retention** | Monthly active usage and return rate over 6, 12, and 24-month windows. |
| **Decision Confidence** | User-reported confidence in decisions made with MR:EGO assistance. |
| **System Reliability** | Uptime, response time, and error rate across all platform services. |
| **Accessibility Compliance** | WCAG compliance level maintained across all interfaces (target: AA minimum, AAA where possible). |
| **Net Promoter Score** | User willingness to recommend MR:EGO to peers. |
| **Feature Adoption** | Percentage of users engaging with new features within 30 days of release. |
| **Platform Expansion** | Number of active integrations, modules, and ecosystem participants. |

## Principles

1. **The User is the Hero:** MR:EGO is the guide, never the protagonist.
2. **One Job Per Screen:** Every view has exactly one primary purpose.
3. **Progress is Visible:** Users always know where they are, what happened, and what is next.
4. **Errors are Opportunities:** Every error message teaches or guides, never blames or shames.
5. **Data has Gravity:** Information persists, connects, and compounds in value over time.
6. **Privacy is Paramount:** Share nothing without permission. Expose nothing by default.
7. **Performance is a Feature:** Speed is not a technical detail; it is a UX requirement.
8. **Offline is Not Optional:** Core functionality works without connectivity.
9. **Consistency is Freedom:** Predictable patterns free users to focus on their work.
10. **Evolution is Continuous:** The platform updates without disruption. Change is seamless.

## What MR:EGO IS

- A unified professional workspace and career operating system
- An AI-augmented decision support platform
- A single source of truth for career and professional activity data
- A modular, extensible ecosystem of integrated capabilities
- A trusted guardian of professional aspirations and personal data
- A platform that grows with the user from individual to enterprise

## What MR:EGO IS NOT

- A social network — connection serves professional purpose, not engagement metrics
- A replacement for human judgment — AI advises, humans decide
- A job board — job discovery is one feature of a much larger career ecosystem
- A project management tool — project management is a module, not the product
- A content platform — content serves career growth, not page views
- A recruiting platform — MR:EGO empowers professionals, not recruiters
- An AI agent that acts autonomously — every consequential action requires human approval
- A design fad — this platform is built for decades, not quarters
- A database of resumes — career data is living, structured, and interconnected
- A productivity tracker — trust is earned, not monitored
